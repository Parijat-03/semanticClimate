The proportion of sunlight (solar radiation) reflected by a surface or object, often expressed as a percentage.

Clouds, snow and ice usually have high albedo; soil surfaces cover the albedo range from high to low; vegetation in the dry season and/or in arid zones can have high albedo, whereas photosynthetically active vegetation and the ocean have low albedo.
