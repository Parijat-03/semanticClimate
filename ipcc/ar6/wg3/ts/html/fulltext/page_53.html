Box TS.6 Figure 1 legend: Limiting warming to a specific level can be consistent with a range of dates when 4 net zero CO2emissions need to be achieved. This difference in the date of net zero CO2emissions reflects the 5 different emissions profiles that are possible while staying within a specific carbon budget and the associated 6 warming limit. Shifting the year of net zero to a later point in time (>2050), however, requires more rapid and 7 deeper near-term emissions reductions (in 2030 and 2040) if warming is to be limited to the same level. 8
*Note: in this assessment the terms net zero CO2emissions and carbon neutrality have different meanings and are 9 only equivalent at the global scale. At the scale of regions, or sectors, each term applies different system 10 boundaries. This is also the case for the related terms net zero GHG and GHG neutrality. {Cross-Chapter Box 3 11 in Chapter 3} 12
