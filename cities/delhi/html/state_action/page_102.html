The strategic focus of the XII FYP for Delhi Transco Limited (DTL) is to renovate and modernized the existing network using modern technology such as GIS and underground cables. Delhi is one of the first new utilities in India to operationalize SCADA and to introduce intrastate Availability Based Tarriff (ABT) metering.
