The new 3 generation of Earth system models, such as CESM2 (included in CMIP6), have been updated with improved 4 snow-layer scheme to represent better polar conditions (Lenaerts et al., 2016). Surface melting is still 5 considerably overestimated in CESM2 compared to RCMs and satellite products, because of the reduced 6 potential for refreezing and overestimation of the net energy available for melt (Trusel et al., 2015; Lenaerts 7 et al., 2016).
