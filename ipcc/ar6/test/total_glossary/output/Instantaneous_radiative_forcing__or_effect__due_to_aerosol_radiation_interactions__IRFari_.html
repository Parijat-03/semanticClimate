instantaneous radiative forcing due to aerosol–radiation interactions

The radiative forcing (or radiative effect, if the perturbation is internally generated) of an aerosol perturbation due directly to aerosol–radiation interactions, with all environmental variables remaining unaffected.

Traditionally known in the literature as the direct aerosol forcing (or effect).
