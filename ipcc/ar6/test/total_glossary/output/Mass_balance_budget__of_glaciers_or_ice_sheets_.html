mass balance/budget

Difference between the mass input (accumulation) and the mass loss (ablation) of an ice body (e.g., a glacier or ice sheet) over a stated time period, which is often a year or a season.

Surface mass balance refers to the difference between surface accumulation and surface ablation.

Sub-terms
  • Ablation (of glaciers, ice sheets, or snow cover)
  • Accumulation (of glaciers, ice sheets or snow cover)
  • Discharge (of ice)
