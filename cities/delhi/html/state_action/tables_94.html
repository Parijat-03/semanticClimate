0 Perform, Achieve and Trade (PAT) is a market based mechanism to enhance cost
1 effectiveness of improvements in energy efficiency in energy intensive large industries and
2 facilities, through certification on energy savings that could be traded. Designated
3 Consumers are obligated to improve energy efficiency & under this scheme a target is given
4 to every DC. Target under PAT scheme is defined as the % reduction of ‘Specific Energy
5 Consumption (SEC)’ from Baseline value. Under PAT-I there are four Designated
6 Consumers in Delhi namely Badarpur Thermal Power Station (BTPS), Rajghat Thermal
7 Power Station (RTPS), Gas Turbine Power Station (GTPS) and Pragati Power Station.
8 EE&REM Centre has successfully completed the monitoring and verification (M&V) of
9 all DCs under PAT-I Cycle. Three new sectors have been added as DCs namely, Refineries,
10 Railways, DISCOMS under PAT Cycle II.
