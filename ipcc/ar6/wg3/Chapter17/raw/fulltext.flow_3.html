3
risks and addressing sustainable development priorities, including water, food and human
4

5

6

7

8

Looking at climate change from a justice perspective means placing the emphasis on a) the protection
10
of vulnerable populations and low income countries from the impacts of climate change, b) mitigating
11
the effects of the transformations, and c) ensuring an equitable decarbonized world {17.1.1}.
