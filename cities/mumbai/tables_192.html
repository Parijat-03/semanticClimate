0 Environment Protection and Pollution Control
1 • The Department will work towards improving information on air pollution through robust consistent monitoring.\n• The Department will monitor and reduce water pollution and work towards restoring aquatic ecosystems (with\nMSDP).\n• The Department will monitor and work towards preventing soil and groundwater pollution by reducing landfilled\nwaste and remediation and scientific management of existing landfills (with SWM Dept).\n• The Department will work towards synergizing the MCAP with other initiatives such as MVA and NCAP.\n• The Department will be responsible for conducting environment impact assessments and coordinating with\nMPCB.

0 Climate Resilient Buildings
1 • The Department will address demand-side solutions for energy conservation by meeting Mumbai’s energy\nefficiency goals for all buildings by 2030.\n• The Department will introduce DCR amendments, enforce green building norms for new buildings, and introduce\nguidelines and trainings to ensure passive architecture and passive cooling is integrated and enforced as part of\nbuilding approvals.\n• The Department will coordinate with Development Planning and Building Approvals within BMC and DISCOMS\nand other stakeholders.

0 Integrated Mobility
1 • The Department will address the mobility needs of city residents and workers with an integrated approach,\nensuring land use and DCR recommendations wherever appropriate.\n• The Department will identify priority “issues” or goals/targets laid out in MCAP and coordinate across all\nparastatal agencies to ensure a human-centred approach to mobility planning in the city.\n• The Department will adopt AI/ML technologies to increase efficiency, increase access and equity in underserved\nneighbourhoods, and influence travel behavior.\n• The Department will introduce finance tools to ease the transition to EV or clean fuel technologies by ensuring\neasy low-interest loans, etc. to assist IPT operators and freight to transition to low-carbon vehicles.
