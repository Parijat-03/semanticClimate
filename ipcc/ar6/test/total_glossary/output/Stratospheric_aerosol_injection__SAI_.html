Stratospheric aerosol injection (SAI) « WGI »

One of several solar radiation modification (SRM) approaches to increase the planetary albedo.

In the approach, it is proposed to inject highly reflective aerosols such as sulphates into the lower stratosphere. This is expected to increase the fraction of solar radiation deflected to space resulting in a planetary cooling.
