Globally, Gross Domestic Product (GDP) per capita and population growth remained the 21 strongest drivers of CO2 emissions from fossil fuel combustion in the last decade (high confidence). 22 Trends since 1990 continued in the years 2010 to 2019 with GDP per capita and population growth 23 increasing emissions by 2.3% and 1.2% yr-1, respectively.
