“Direct Mitigation Focus” {2.8, 13.6} Objective: Reduce GHG emissions now Literature: How to design and implement policy instruments, with attention to distributional and other concerns Examples: Carbon tax, cap and trade, border carbon adjustment, disclosure policies

Objective: Accelerate low-carbon shifts in socio-technical systems Literature: Understand socio-technical transition processes, integrated policies for different stages of a technology ‘S curve’ and explore structural, social and political elements of transitions Examples: Packages for renewable energy transition and coal phase-out; diffusion of electric vehicles, process and fuel switching in key industries
