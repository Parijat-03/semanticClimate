model spread

The range or spread in results from climate models, such as those assembled for Coupled Model Intercomparison Project Phase 6 (CMIP6).

Does not necessarily provide an exhaustive and formal estimate of the uncertainty in feedbacks, forcing or projections even when expressed numerically, for example, by computing a standard deviation of the modelsâ€™ responses. In order to quantify uncertainty, information from observations, physical constraints and expert judgement must be combined, using a statistical framework.
