The assessment of feasibility in this report aims to identify barriers and enablers to the deployment of 4 mitigation options and pathways. The assessment organises evidence to support policy decisions, and 5 decisions on actions, that would improve the feasibility of mitigation options and pathways by removing 6 relevant barriers and by strengthening enablers of change. 7
The feasibility of mitigation response options 8
Mitigation response options are assessed against six dimensions of feasibility. Each dimension 9 comprises a key set of indicators that can be evaluated by combining various strands of literature. 10 {Annex II Part IV Section 11, Table 6.1} 11
