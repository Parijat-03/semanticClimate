Very high risks to low-latitude yields may occur between 3°C and 4°C (medium conﬁ dence). At these temperatures, catastrophic reductions in crop
yields may occur, of up to 60% in low latitudes (Rosenzweig et al. 2014) (Sections 5.2.2 and 5.2.3). Some studies report signiﬁ cant population displacement from the tropics related to systemic livelihood disruption in agriculture systems (Tittonell 2014; Montaña et al. 2016; Huber-Sannwald et al. 2012; Wise et al. 2016; Tanner et al. 2015; Mohapatra 2013).
