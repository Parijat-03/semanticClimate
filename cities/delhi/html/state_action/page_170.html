Delhi’s power demand has increased continuously and the dependence on the power from outside has been increasing simultaneously. Though, the power corporations have earned sufficient surpluses during the winter season when Delhi has power-surplus, the summer peaks are way high above the total arranged capacity which leads to load shedding. The peak electricity demand has touched the level of 5,150 MW in Delhi in May, 201231. At present Delhi has 25.5 MW grid connected Installed Capacity of Renewable Power. The CEA estimates indicate that electricity demand may reach to around 8700 MW in Delhi by the end of the 12th Five Year Plan.
