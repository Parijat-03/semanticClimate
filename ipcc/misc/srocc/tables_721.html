3 More than 50 countries have developed national REDD+ strategies, which have key conditions for addressing deforestation and forest\ndegradation (improved monitoring capacities, understanding of drivers, increased stakeholder involvement, and providing a platform\nto secure indigenous and community land rights). However, to achieve its original objectives and to be effective under current\nconditions, forest-based mitigation actions need to be incorporated in national development plans and official climate strategies, and\nmainstreamed across sectors and levels of government (Angelsen et al. 2018a).
