Northern Australia (NAU) 40 which is mainly tropical with monsoonal summer-dominated rainfall, but with a hot, semi-arid climate in the 41 south of the region; Central and Eastern Australia (CAU) with a hot, dry desert climate in the west and 42 centre, ranging to a temperate climate in the east;
