5.Initiatives by Delhi Government
Delhi Government had initially drafted an action plan for Climate Change which got expired in 20129. It broadly follows the NAPCC except two aspects of Himalayan Ecosystem and Agriculture and is adapted to urban challenges of Delhi as an emerging global metropolis. The modified Climate Change Agenda for Delhi 2009-12 has put forth 36 key actions items that the Government intends to undertake in the coming years. The agenda contains the framework for implementation of a series of action by various stakeholders.
