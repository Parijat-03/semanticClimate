Box TS.15 | A Harmonised Approach to Assessing Feasibility
The assessment of feasibility in this report aims to identify barriers and enablers to the deployment of mitigation options and pathways. The assessment organises evidence to support policy decisions, and decisions on actions, that would improve the feasibility
The feasibility of mitigation response optionsMitigation response options are assessed against six dimensions of feasibility. Each dimension comprises a key set of indicators that can be evaluated by combining various strands of literature. {Annex II.IV.11, Table 6.1}
