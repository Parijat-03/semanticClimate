Plankton « WGI,WGII »

Free-floating organisms living in the upper layers of aquatic systems.

Their distribution and migration are primarily determined by water currents. A distinction is made between phytoplankton, which depend on photosynthesis for their energy supply, and zooplankton, which feed on phytoplankton, other zooplankton and bacterioplankton.
