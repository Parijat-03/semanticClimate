Action 5: Facilitate capacity building for the advancement of research on climate change. This action point will focus on facilitating capacity building initiatives to further the skillsets of government officials in the state, on application of climate science, GIS and remote sensing applications, Hydrological modelling etc. through the network of national and international knowledge institutes. This would also include orientation sessions and workshops on climate change for government officials to better understand climate change dynamics and impacts, along with opportunities and issues related to adaptation and mitigation.
