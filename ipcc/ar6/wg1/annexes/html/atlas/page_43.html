For the 2021–2060 period, a pronounced increase of 0.27℃ to 0.51℃ per 38 decade is projected for temperature while precipitation change is estimated to be between –1.7% to 6.8% per 39 decade varying seasonally and spatially; a decrease of precipitation emerged in the western region of 40 Xinjiang during summer.
