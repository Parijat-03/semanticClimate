Calcification « WGII,WGI »

The process of biologically precipitating calcium carbonate minerals to create organism shells, skeletons, otoliths, or other body structures.

The chemical equation describing calcification is Ca2+(aq) + 2HCO3−(aq) → CaCO3(s) + CO2 + H2O. Aragonite and calcite are two common crystalline forms of biologically precipitated calcium carbonate minerals that have different solubilities.
