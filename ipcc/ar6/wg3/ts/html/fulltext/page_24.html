Globally, households with income in the top 10% contribute about 36-45% of global GHG 17 emissions (robust evidence, medium agreement). About two thirds of the top 10% live in developed 18 countries and one third in other economies. The lifestyle consumption emissions of the middle income 19 and poorest citizens in emerging economies are between 5-50 times below their counterparts in high-20 income countries (medium confidence).
