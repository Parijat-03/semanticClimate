participatory governance

A governance system that enables direct public engagement in decision-making using a variety of techniques, for example, referenda, community deliberation, citizen juries or participatory budgeting.

The approach can be applied in formal and informal institutional contexts from national to local, but is usually associated with devolved decision making (Fung and Wright, 2003; Sarmiento and Tilly, 2018).
