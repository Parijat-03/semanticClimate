Despite all efforts in researching, analysing and understanding climate change, a 14 key factor in the spatial scales at which analysis can be undertaken is directly related to the availability and 15 reliability of data, both observational and from model simulations. In addition, information is required over a 16 wide range of spatial domains defined either from a climatological or geographical perspective (e.g., a region 17 affected by monsoon rainfall or a river basin) or from a socio-economic or political perspective (e.g., least-18 developed countries or nation states).
