Delhi Government also launched its first Car-Free-Day on 22ndOctober 2015 with Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal leading the Cycle Rally with over 1,000 cyclists took part in the event which encouraged people tous public transport.
A number of additional measures have been undertaken to reduce vehicular pollution by providing CNG buses, strengthening the Pollution under Control (PUC) system by online networking, introduction of strict Inspection and Certification Systems and promotion of advanced technologies. Special focus is on safety certification and stringent inspection of all transport vehicles. A Safety Council to set standards for vehicle safety and fitness is being set up shortly.
