6.4CNG Consumption
The Compressed natural Gas (CNG) is mostly used for the purpose of transportation in Delhi. The tremendous shift in fuel usage and the adoption of CNG as a transportation fuel has not only improved the level of pollution but also has resulted in the improved air quality in Delhi. GNCTD has mandated the compulsory use of CNG in all the commercial vehicles plying on the roads of Delhi. As a result of this enforcement, the pollution level has drastically reduced and the air quality has improved a lot. The CNG is not only a clean fuel but is also brought an option of cheap fuel to the customers. The CNG consumption in Delhi as given by Indraprastha Gas Limited (IGL) is 75.61 lakhs kilograms per day. The total sale of CNG as reported by IGL is 75.61 lakhs kilograms per day. Total number of CNG stations in Delhi is 324 of which 58 are mother stations, 245 are online stations, 21 are daughter/daughter booster stations. Total numbers of vehicles operating on CNG are 438,900. The figure 12 classifies the type of vehicles operating on CNG.
