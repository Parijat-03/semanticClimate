Snow cover « WGI,WGII »

Snow cover refers to all the snow that has accumulated on the ground at a given time (UNESCO/IASH/WMO, 1970).

Sub-terms
  • Snow cover duration (SCD)
  • Snow cover extent (SCE)
  • Snow water equivalent (SWE)
References
- UNESCO/IASH/WMO Technical papers in hydrology 1970. Seasonal Snow Cover, a guide for measurement, compilation and assemblage of data
