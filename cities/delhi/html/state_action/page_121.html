In order to improve the quality of forests, Department of Forests and Wildlife has developed 42 City Forests and woodlots. Gradually these areas are being opened for the public to ensure better management and creation of awareness for environmental values.
Recorded Forest (Notified Forest Areas in Delhi) About 305.41 sq. km. of Delhi is covered with forest and trees which is 20.59% of the total area.24The total recorded forest area in Delhi is 102 sq. km i.e. 6.88% of the geographic area of which the Reserved and Protected Forests constitute 76.48% and 23.52% of the total forest area respectively.
Reserved Forest “Ridge” which is the rocky outcrop of Aravali hills in Delhi, has been notified as Reserved Forests under Section 4 of the Indian Forest Act, 1927 vide Notification No.F.10 (42)-1/PA/DCF/93/2012-17(I) dated 24th May, 1994. The Hon’ble Supreme Court, through various orders passed in Writ Petition (Civil) No. 4677/1985 (MC Mehta vs. UOI & Ors.) has directed that ridge should be maintained in its pristine glory.
