3 Urban areas have special interactions with the climate system, for instance in terms of heat islands and altering the water cycle, and\nthereby will be more affected by extreme climate events such as extreme heat (high confidence). With global warming, increasing\nrelative sea level compounded by increasing tropical cyclone storm surge and rainfall intensity will increase the probability of coastal\ncity flooding (high confidence). Arctic coastal settlements are particularly exposed to climate change due to sea ice retreat (high\nconfidence). Improvements in urban climate modelling and climate monitoring networks have contributed to understanding the\nmutual interaction between regional and urban climate (high confidence). {Box 10.3, 11.3, 11.5, 12.3, 12.4}
