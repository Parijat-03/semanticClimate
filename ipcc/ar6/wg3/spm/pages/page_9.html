The full GHG emissions impact of the COVID-19 pandemic could not be assessed due to a lack of data regarding non-CO2GHG emissions in 2020. {Cross-Chapter Box 1 in Chapter 1, Figure 2.6, 2.2, Box TS.1, Box TS.1 Figure 1}
