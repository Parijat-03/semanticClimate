9.2Existing Concerns in Energy & Power Sector
The power reforms were undertaken in Delhi for improving the sector in the areas of supply side and making the sector viable by reducing AT&C losses which was about 50%. After unbundling on 01.07.2002, there is significant improvement. The AT&C losses have been brought down to around 15% in many of the areas. The load shedding during 2001-02 prior to the reforms was to the tune of 5% of the supply has been reduced to 0.13% in 2015-16. This factor is significant when the energy consumption has shown an increase of 29.8% from year 2009-10 to 2015-16.
