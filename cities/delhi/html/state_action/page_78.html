8.2City Level GHG Emission Inventory
The tCO2e emission load in the city is estimated to be 37.91 million tonne for the year 2014. The top four contributors to tCO2e emission are Power plants (43%), Vehicles (32%), MSW burning (8%) and domestic cooking (7%); these are based on annual emissions. Seasonal and daily emissions could be variable. For example, MSW burning is more frequent in winter than in summer. The estimated emission suggests that there are many important sources and a composite emission abatement including most of the sources will be required to reduce the GHG emission.
CH4 emission in the city is estimated to be 37078 tonne/yr. The top three contributors to CH4 emissions are livestock (61%), vehicles (18 %), and landfills (9%); these are based on annual emissions. Seasonal and daily emissions could be variable. N2O emissions are lower than CH4 emission ~ 7794 t/d. nearly 92% of emissions are attributed to vehicular emissions.
