Flood Prone Areas – Propensity to flood may serve to increase the vulnerability of a given area to impacts of climate change. According to the Delhi flood control order, Delhi has been divided into four flood sectors. These include Shahadra, Wazirabad – Babrapur, Alipur and Nangloi – Najafgarh (Census, 2011).
